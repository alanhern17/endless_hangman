How to Play

1. Classic hangman with a twist! Guess the random words one letter at a time while trying to achieve a high score.

2. You will get points based on the length of the word and your point multiplier.

3. Your point multiplier increases everytime you guess a word correctly and your strikes reset.

4. Guessing a letter wrong will cost you a strike, and a part of the hangman will be added.

5. Run out of strikes and it's game over!
